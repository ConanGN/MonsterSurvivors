About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where you must battle against endless waves of monsters. As time progresses, the difficulty increases, forcing you to adapt your strategy and evolve your character to survive.

Inspired by popular roguelike games, Monster Survivors combines fast-paced action with strategic character building. Each playthrough offers a unique experience as you discover different power-ups and face increasingly challenging enemies.

Upgrades

Tips & Strategies

Master Monster Survivors with these essential tips:

  1. Keep moving - Standing still is a quick way to get surrounded.
  2. Focus on synergistic upgrades - Choose upgrades that complement each other rather than spreading across different ability types.
  3. Prioritize enemies - Some monsters are more dangerous than others. Learn to identify threats and deal with them first.
  4. Collect experience gems - Level up quickly by actively seeking out experience drops from defeated enemies.
  5. Use the terrain - Some maps have obstacles that can help you funnel and control enemy movements.

Remember that each run will be different. Don't be discouraged by failure—learn from each attempt and try new strategies!
